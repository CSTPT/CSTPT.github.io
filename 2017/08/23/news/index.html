AMA運動員動作評估是什麼？

This system is a criterion-based assessment. We do not tabulate and grade categorically someone’s movement. It is not possible first of all and secondly, with the concept of movement variability, which is a focal point to understanding movement in our paradigm, movement is constantly evolving; which consequently cannot be said to be truly “dysfunctional” when you may not have a true comparison. This is most especially true in the developing athlete.

AMA這套評估系統依循了特定的準則，我們不會對於個別動作評分，因為這是不可行的；其次，動作變異性的概念是本體系的重點，由於動作本身會持續不斷的變化，在無從比較下我們無法認定是否真的失能，這對於發展中的運動員來說特別重要。

This course will help guide your training programs and rehabilitation so that each exercise/drill prescribed has a specific intent based off of the findings from the assessment. The Athletic Movement Assessment (AMA) courses will not only teach how to assess the athlete/client/patient, but it will also teach what to do after the assessment is complete.

本課程將會指導學員，如何設計適當的訓練或復健計畫，根據評估的結果去設計各項符合特定需要的運動或動作。AMA研習課程不只是教導如何評估運動員/客戶/患者，也會教導評估之後該如何處置。

The assessment is designed to establish, using augmented feedback and external cuing, what the potential underlying issue(s) is/are that would in part explain the poor movement in the patient-athlete/client. Many of the movement protocols people perform are patterns unfamiliar and or are not learned or once were and now lack understanding. In essence we are trying to establish where on the spectrum of motor skill acquisition do we need to intervene: therapeutically vs motor learning (rehab strategies) vs. advanced exercise regimes (performance).

本套評估系統將教育我們，如何利用 強化回饋 或 外部提示去發現，可能造成運動員或患者不良動作的潛在問題，許多動作的問題來自於不熟悉動作模式，包含沒有學習過此動作模式，或是曾經會但現在失去了此動作能力，我們將AMA系統應用在動作技能學習領域上所需要介入的範圍：治療/動作學習(復健策略)/進階運動(運動表現)。

There is no algorithm but only a thought process to assessing the movement. The skills people learn: understand what dysfunction and motor learning deficits are because they are not the same and simply labeling dysfunction is inappropriate and not supported in the literature.

AMA沒有固定的公式，而是提供評估動作的思考過程，你將會了解 失能 與 動作學習障礙 的差異性，單純的認定失能，並不合適且沒有文獻證據支持這項作法。

We have attempted to bring the science to explain how and what we do to best train the client/athlete.

我們嘗試藉由科學的方式去解釋，如何以最佳的方式訓練客戶/運動員。
